FAQs
📖 How does Algora work?
▾
Every government fund transfer is recorded on the Algorand blockchain — a permanent, tamper-proof public ledger. No one can edit or delete a transaction once it's confirmed.
🔐 Is my Aadhar data safe?
▾
We never store your raw Aadhar number. Only a verified hash is saved — we confirm you're a real citizen without ever seeing your personal details.
🔗 How do I verify a transaction?
▾
Click any transaction hash — it opens directly on the Algorand blockchain explorer where you can verify it independently, without trusting Algora at all.
